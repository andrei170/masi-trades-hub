01

Research Snapshot

6 gaps identified across website, funnel, and distribution

The Six Gaps

  • No VSL on course page. Trading education is a high-trust purchase. A 3-5 minute video with broker statements and student proof would compress the trust cycle from days to minutes. Every major trading educator running paid traffic uses a VSL.
  • In-person training limits scalability. Highest-ticket tier caps revenue at venue capacity and physical presence. No hybrid delivery, no recorded cohort component. A hybrid model would serve 3-5x more students.
  • No visible email list or lead magnet. Funnel goes straight from IG to paid product. At $1,500-$5,000 price points, a warmup layer is mandatory. An Options Trading Starter Guide captures emails and feeds a 25-day nurture.
  • No YouTube for long-form education. 123K on IG, zero YouTube. Options education needs chart breakdowns and live trade walkthroughs that require 10-20 minutes. YouTube is the primary discovery engine for trading education searches.
  • No clear pricing on website. Likely DM-based. A price-hinting approach reduces unqualified DM volume and increases application quality from serious buyers.
  • Only two visible tiers. The gap between Online Course ($1,500-$2,000) and In-Person ($5,000+) leaves money on the table. A group coaching tier at $3,000-$4,000 fills that gap.

Highest-Leverage Fix

Build a short VSL for the course page plus an email capture with lead magnet. These two additions alone would add a warmup layer and compress trust-building for every prospect who visits the site.
